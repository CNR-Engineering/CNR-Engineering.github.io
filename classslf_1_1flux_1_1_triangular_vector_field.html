Detailed Description

The representation of Mesh2D in Serafin file when one computes the flux across sections of some vectors.

The integral of scalars along a line can also be computed.

The flux across a section (or integral along a line) is the sum of the flux (or integral) on all intersected segments between the mesh and the section.
